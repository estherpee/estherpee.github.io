Hip-Hop Holiday Colouring Book

  • Role: Illustration, Creative Direction
  • Year: 2016

A hip-hop themed colouring book for the holidays.

Two friends and I wanted to try and make something fun for the holidays, so we made and sold hip-hop themed colouring books with a christmas twist. The book has 20 pages of funny illustrations of rappers doing Christmas-themed things, and 10 pages of hip-hop themed patterns. There is something for everyone in this book, whether you're a casual hip-hop fan or a hip-hop head.

We designed, produced, marketed, and sold the books in the span of 3 weeks, and had sales coming from all over Canada, the United States, Korea, Australia, and the Netherlands.
